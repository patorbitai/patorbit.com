Challenge

Turn incoming enquiries into clear next steps

New leads often arrive through forms, email, chat, or WhatsApp. Without a clear process, good opportunities are missed and low-fit enquiries consume too much time.

Solution

A qualification workflow that prepares every follow-up

Lead intake

Collects submissions from contact forms, spreadsheets, inboxes, or simple CRM tools.

Intent scoring

Scores urgency, budget signal, service fit, and project type using transparent rules.

Draft response

Prepares a personalized follow-up message, question list, and recommended next action.

Tracker update

Writes qualified leads, scores, notes, and status updates into a simple operating view.

Tools

Automation building blocks

Form intake AI classification Follow-up drafts CRM or sheet sync
Benefits

What the automation delivers

  • Faster response time for serious enquiries
  • Less manual sorting of low-fit requests
  • Consistent follow-up notes and next actions
  • A simple sales operations workflow that can scale
Build Approach

Keep humans in control of important replies

The workflow prepares the lead summary and suggested response, but a person can review before sending.

This makes the first launch practical for businesses that want speed without losing judgment.

Launch checklist

  • Define lead stages and qualification rules
  • Connect the source where enquiries arrive
  • Test scoring against real past leads
  • Review drafts before automating final sends
