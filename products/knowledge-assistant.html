Challenge

Make internal knowledge easier to use

Teams lose time searching old files, chat messages, policies, notes, and process documents. The assistant turns scattered information into answers people can actually act on.

Solution

A controlled assistant for documents, processes, and team memory

Document Q&A

Answers questions from approved documents and points users to the source material when needed.

Smart summaries

Condenses long notes, policy files, proposals, and reports into useful summaries.

Process guidance

Guides users through repeatable steps such as onboarding, approvals, reporting, or customer follow-up.

Reusable outputs

Drafts checklists, emails, meeting notes, and next-action summaries in a consistent format.

Features

Assistant capabilities

Knowledge retrieval Document summaries Process answers Source-aware responses
Benefits

What this assistant delivers

  • Less time spent searching repeated information
  • Clearer answers from approved internal material
  • Reusable drafts and summaries for daily work
  • A practical foundation for internal AI adoption
Build Approach

Start small, then expand the knowledge base

The first version can focus on a single function, such as support docs, onboarding material, sales FAQs, or operating procedures.

Once the workflow is trusted, more documents, roles, and output formats can be added.

Launch checklist

  • Choose the first document set and target users
  • Define what the assistant should refuse or escalate
  • Test answers against real team questions
  • Review usage and improve the source material
