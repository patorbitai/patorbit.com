Building Practical AI Systems With Clear Business Value

Profile

A practical AI product studio

PAT Orbit works at the intersection of AI, automation, and lightweight software delivery. The focus is not on hype; it is on tools people can actually use.

Every build starts with a real task: answering customers, qualifying leads, preparing documents, summarizing data, routing requests, or helping a team move faster.

What PAT Orbit Cares About

  • AI agents that solve a specific job
  • Automation that reduces repetitive manual work
  • Software products that are simple to test and improve
  • Human oversight where accuracy and trust matter
