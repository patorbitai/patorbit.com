Challenge

Give HR teams fast visibility into workforce trends

HR leaders need a single view of employee tenure, attrition signals, and department health, without wading through spreadsheets or disconnected reports.

Solution

A dashboard built for HR operations and leadership

Employee headcount

Tracks total workforce size, new hires, and departures over time.

Attrition insights

Highlights attrition rates by department, role, and tenure segment.

Team performance

Shows productivity and key HR metrics for departments and business units.

Workforce demographics

Analyzes experience, tenure, and role composition across the organization.
